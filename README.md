# About

Attack on Titan is a dark fantasy series where humanity is forced to live within giant walled cities to hide from man-eating giants called Titans. The story follows Eren Yeager and his friends who join the military to reclaim their world after a tragic breach of their home. As the conflict escalates, the plot shifts from a simple survival story into a complex political drama involving deep history and moral gray areas. It is widely praised for its intense action, high-stakes mystery, and a soundtrack that heightens the emotional weight of every battle. Ultimately, the series explores themes of freedom and the cyclical nature of war through its shocking twists and high-impact animation.
